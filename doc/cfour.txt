*cfour.txt*	        Plugin for CFOUR <https://cfour.uni-mainz.de/cfour/>

This plugin offers highlighting of some of CFOUR's ZMAT syntax and also some
other stuff.

Mappings:
<Leader>e   or   <Plug>CfourShow
	Show the molecular geometry in jmol. Jmol needs to be installed and be
	visible in $PATH. xjoda (part of CFOUR) also has to be visible.

						*cfour-settings*
This plugin doesn't have any settings.
